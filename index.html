ABOUT ME

Who am I ?

I’m Zanele(19), a girl who’s still figuring life out but choosing to grow through every season. I’m curious, intentional, and a little stubborn in the best way. I love tech, meaningful conversations, quiet mornings, and doing things that push me out of my comfort zone I’m not perfect, but I’m real, and that’s my superpower.

My Extra-Curriculars

Outside of school, I’m a little bit of everything — and I love it that way. I’m a Miss Teenager South Africa FINALIST, creating Empowerment Friday content and sharpening my voice as a youth advocate. I also read a lot, because books are my escape. I draw when I want to get out of my head and express myself creatively. And when it comes to sports… I swear I’m sporty in my heart, even though I’ve never actually joined a team — but the enthusiasm is there. I’m exploring, creating, learning, and doing things that make life feel full and exciting.

Impactful Things I’ve Done

I’ve spoken openly about identity struggles, academic pressure, and the challenges young people face. I advocate for the Unseen Generation, a movement that stands for the youth that feels unseen, unheard, unsupported and overlooked and I use my platform to make others feel seen and understood. I’ve volunteered at the Boksburg SPCA, and at Leratong children home, I became a guest speaker at QwaQwa radio, I have done multiple school visits around the Free State province, created empowering content that inspires others, and committed myself to becoming a positive influence — not just online, but in real life too. I also created a website for the unseen generation.
